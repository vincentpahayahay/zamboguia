Modernjeep

The modern jeepneys are air-conditioned and are provisioned for standing in, so there’s room for passengers who choose to stand during commutes. With that, its 26-passenger capacity can be maximized further.

It’s more of a minibus, though, than a conventional "king of the road.” It’s also bigger than most jeepneys

As for the ride comfort, modern jeepneys are a whole lot better than the old jeepneys. The seats are well-cushioned and there’s ample wiggle room for each passenger. Even better, daily commutes would be much cozier with its air-conditioning.

Hail the modern jeepney like how you would on a traditional jeepney.
